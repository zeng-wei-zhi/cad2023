Creo Overview

Creo provides students the opportunity to work with state-of-the-art 3D CAD software, enabling them to take ideas and concepts and turn them into products. Creo offers the best preparation to become an Engineer of the Future, by putting design, analysis, and simulation tools directly in the hands of students.
 

Installation and System Requirements

  • OS: Windows 10 on 64 bit
  • Memory – Minimum 4GB RAM
  • Hardware Requirement – 3 button mouse
  • Learn about additional requirements here
  • Interested in cloud-native CAD that works on any device? Try Onshape.
